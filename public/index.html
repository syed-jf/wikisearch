About WikiSearch

WikiSearch is an intelligent knowledge search engine designed to help you seamlessly explore, synthesize, and delve into the vast lattice of human knowledge.

Core Functionalities

  • Interactive Synthesis: Ask follow-up questions and explore any concept with structured AI summaries.
  • Voice Search: Seamlessly search hands-free using built-in speech recognition.
  • Philosopher's Diary: Access dynamic scholarly digests and tailored book recommendations.
  • Inquiry History: Easily track, manage, or clear your past learning sessions.
